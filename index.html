about

Alex Ogden is a graduate student at Minneapolis College of Art and Design where she studies graphic and web design. She enjoys nerding out over code and experimenting with unconventional design.

Alex is also an illustrator, photographer, musician, and unabashed admirer of all things cat. She launched Forever Fox design in 2018.
